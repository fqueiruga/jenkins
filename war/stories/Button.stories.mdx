## Jelly utilities

### <f:submit\>

Create a submit button to be used in forms. Examples:

`<f:submit name="yes" value="${%Tell me more}" />`
`<f:submit value="${%Save}" large="true" />`

<Preview>
  <Story name="Jelly: <f:submit>">
    {() => `
      <input type="submit"
        name="yes"
        value="Tell me more"
        class="primary" />
      <input type="submit"
        name="Submit"
        value="Save"
        class="primary large-button" />
    `}
  </Story>
</Preview>
